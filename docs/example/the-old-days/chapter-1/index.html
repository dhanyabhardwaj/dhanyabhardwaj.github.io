    The Letter #

    It was the usual sunny day in London. The streets were filled with people and the shops were out of clothes, food, furniture etc. Nearby, a small house was covered with thornes and ivy looked like it was abandoned .beside the abandoned house was a place almost like a mansion just a bit smaller it was all white and had a beautiful garden in the middle of the garden was a statue of a little boy with a crown on his head.

    Inside the mansion

    Joe just woke up and put his glasses on he slipped his feet in his slippers and jogged down stairs. When he got down he saw his dad with a cup of tea in his hand plus the newspaper his mum was in the kitchen talking to her sister on the phone. Joe picked up his breakfast and went to sit with his dad on the sofa .Joe turned on the TV to see the news ,well the weather he and his sister Ava were going to Joes friends house for a sleep over just then Ava came hopping up and down and in the kitchen.

    Joe was ten and Ava was 3 years younger then him. After breakfast Joe had to go to football club .It took 30 minute to get there.
